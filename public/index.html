Organize your tv shows with one click

Authorize

What it does?

  • It creates -=- TV Series -=- folder
  • It goes through the list of your recent transfers and moves all TV shows to this new folder
  • Each show goes to it's own subfolder
  • Once per hour your transfer list is checked and TV shows are moved

How do I uninstall?

Just go to Account -> AUTHORIZED APPLICATIONS and revoke access.
